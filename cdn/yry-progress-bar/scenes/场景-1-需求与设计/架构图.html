双模式判定架构

flowchart TB
    CE["<yry-progress-bar>"] --> CHECK{"el.hasAttribute('done')
|| el.hasAttribute('total')"}
    CHECK -->|"是"| EXPLICIT["显式任务进度模式
_scrollMode = false
pct = done/total*100"]
    CHECK -->|"否"| SCROLL["自动滚动追踪模式
_scrollMode = true
绑定 scroll + resize"]
    SCROLL --> RAF["RAF 节流
_ticking flag"]
    RAF --> UPDATE["_updateScroll()"]
    EXPLICIT --> RENDER["渲染: pb-wrap > pb-track > pb-fill > pb-shine"]
    UPDATE --> RENDER

Props → 视图映射

flowchart LR
    DONE["done: Number"] --> PCT["computed pct"]
    TOTAL["total: Number"] --> PCT
    LABEL["label: String"] --> ARIA["aria-label"]
    PCT --> WIDTH[":style={width: pct+'%'}"]
    PCT --> DONE_CLASS[":class={ 'is-done': pct>=100 }"]
    PCT --> EMPTY_CLASS[":class={ 'is-empty': pct<=0 }"]
